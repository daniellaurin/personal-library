REVIEWS & RATINGS

SHARE YOUR READING EXPERIENCE

WRITING A REVIEW

You must be logged in to write a review. One review per book per user.

  1. Find the book on the LIBRARY page or via SEARCH.
  2. Click the book card to open its detail page.
  3. Click ⭐ WRITE A REVIEW.
  4. Select your star rating (1 = worst, 5 = best) by clicking the stars.
  5. Choose your Reading Format: Hardcover, Paperback, Ebook, or Audiobook.
  6. Choose your Reading Status: Want to Read, Currently Reading, or Read.
  7. Optionally write a review in the text box.
  8. Click SAVE REVIEW. You will be returned to the book's page.

EDITING YOUR REVIEW

You can edit your review at any time:

  • Open the book's detail page and click ✏️ EDIT YOUR REVIEW.
  • Or go to your PROFILE and click EDIT REVIEW next to any entry.

Editing replaces your existing review - it does not create a duplicate.

READING OPTIONS (PER BOOK)

Each review includes two configurable options specific to your reading experience:

OPTIONCHOICES
READING FORMAT Hardcover · Paperback · Ebook · Audiobook
READING STATUS Want to Read · Currently Reading · Read

VIEWING REVIEWS

On any book's detail page, scroll past the book info to see all community reviews listed newest-first. Each review shows:

  • Reviewer username
  • Star rating (★★★★☆)
  • Reading format and status badges
  • Date written
  • Review text (if provided)

The book detail page also shows the average community rating and total review count.

YOUR READING HISTORY

Visit your PROFILE to see all books you have reviewed, along with your personal reading stats:

  • Total books reviewed
  • Average rating you give
  • Count by status (Read, Currently Reading, Want to Read)
