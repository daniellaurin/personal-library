GETTING STARTED

YOUR FIRST STEPS IN THE LIBRARY

STEP 1 - CREATE AN ACCOUNT

  1. Click REGISTER in the top navigation bar.
  2. Enter a username (letters, numbers, underscores - 3 to 50 characters).
  3. Provide a valid email address.
  4. Create a password of at least 8 characters and confirm it.
  5. Click CREATE ACCOUNT. You will be automatically logged in.

STEP 2 - SIGN IN

  1. Click LOGIN in the navigation bar.
  2. Enter the email and password you registered with.
  3. Click SIGN IN. Your name will appear in the nav on success.

If your account has been disabled by an administrator, contact the site admin to reactivate it.

STEP 3 - EXPLORE THE SITE

Use the navigation bar at the top of every page to move around:

  • HOME - landing page with library stats and recent additions.
  • SEARCH - find books via the Google Books API and add them.
  • LIBRARY - browse the full book collection.
  • AMBIANCE - play ambient sounds while you read.
  • PROFILE - view your personal reviews and reading stats (logged-in users).
  • ADMIN - site management panel (admin users only).
  • HELP - this wiki.

SIGNING OUT

Click LOGOUT in the navigation bar at any time. Your session will be cleared immediately.
