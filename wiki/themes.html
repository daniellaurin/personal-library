SEASONAL THEMES

CHANGE THE LOOK OF THE LIBRARY

HOW TO SWITCH THEMES

Look for the season emoji buttons in the top-right corner of the navigation bar on every page. Click the emoji for the season you want:

BUTTONTHEMECOLOURS
🍂 AUTUMN Warm brown background · Amber accents
🌸 SPRING Deep green background · Pink accents
❄️ WINTER Deep navy background · Ice blue accents

ANIMATED SEASONAL EFFECTS

Each theme includes animated particles that drift across the screen:

  • 🍂 Autumn - leaves spin and drift sideways as they fall.
  • 🌸 Spring - cherry blossom petals float down with a gentle sway.
  • ❄️ Winter - snowflakes drift slowly and steadily downward.

When you switch themes, new particles slide in from the right side of the screen before beginning their fall - a visual transition marking the change of season.

YOUR PREFERENCE IS SAVED

The site remembers your chosen theme across pages and visits using your browser's localStorage. No account is needed - the preference is stored in your browser.

If you have never selected a theme, the site will automatically choose one based on the current calendar month:

  • March – May → Spring
  • September – November → Autumn
  • All other months → Winter

RESETTING YOUR THEME

To reset to the default seasonal theme, open your browser's developer tools, go to Application → Local Storage, and delete the key pl-season. The site will auto-detect the season on next load.
